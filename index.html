I'm a Data Scientist (KTP Associate) in School of Computer Science at the University of Exeter, where I work closely with Dr. Jacqueline Christmas and Prof. Richard Everson on a KTP project. My research interest lies in the area of signal processing and machine learning, specialised in statistical modelling, algorithm development, sensor array processing, signal processing for communications, etc.

Prior to this, I was a Postdoctoral Research Associate at Cardiff University (Apr 2018-May 2019). I received my Ph.D. degree in Signal Processing from Cardiff University in 2017, where I was supervised by Prof. John G. McWhirter and Dr. Yulia Hicks.
