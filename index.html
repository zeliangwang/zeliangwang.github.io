Welcome to my homepage! I'm a Data Scientist in School of Computer Science at the University of Exeter, where I work on a KTP project which aims to identify energy savings and improve energy efficience from electricity metering data in a more intelligent and efficent way by using advanced machine learning and signal processing techniques. My research interests cover a wide range of topics in signal processing and machine learning, specialised in statistical modelling, algorithm development, sensor array processing, signal processing for communications, etc.

Prior to this, I was a Postdoctoral Research Associate at Cardiff University (Apr 2018-May 2019). I received a PhD degree in Signal Processing from Cardiff University in 2017.
