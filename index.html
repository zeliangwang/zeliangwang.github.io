I'm a Data Scientist (KTP Associate) in School of Computer Science at the University of Exeter, where I work closely with Dr. Jacqueline Christmas and Prof. Richard Everson on a KTP project. The broad aim of the KTP project is to improve energy efficience from 'big' metering data in a more intelligent and efficent way by using advanced machine learning and AI techniques. My research interests cover a wide range of topics in signal processing and machine learning, specialised in statistical modelling, algorithm development, sensor array processing, signal processing for communications, etc.

Previously, I received a Ph.D. degree in Signal Processing from Cardiff University, where I was supervised by Prof. John G. McWhirter and Dr. Yulia Hicks. I was a Postdoctoral Research Associate at Cardiff University (Apr 2018-May 2019).
